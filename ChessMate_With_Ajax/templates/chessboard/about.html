About Me

Fatima Sultana

Hello, I'm Fatima Sultana, a master's student in computer science at Chico State with a strong passion for software development, data science, artificial intelligence, and machine learning. I've gained valuable experience through an internship as a web developer, focusing on the LAMP stack, and as a machine learning research assistant at JNTUH, where I worked on natural language processing and recommendation systems. As a Teaching Assistant, I guide students in programming, algorithms, and data structures, enhancing both my teaching and technical skills.

My ultimate goal is to work in the software industry, gain substantial experience, and eventually pursue a PhD, aspiring to become a professor in software engineering or machine learning. Outside of academics, I enjoy hiking, gardening, and playing badminton, which helps me stay active and balanced.
